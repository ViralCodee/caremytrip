Frequently asked questions

What is the best time for the Chardham Yatra?

The Char Dham temples typically open from late April/May and close around Diwali (Oct/Nov). May–June and September–October are the most popular and pleasant windows for the yatra.

Does CareMyTrip offer Chardham Yatra by helicopter?

Yes — our 6-day Chardham Yatra by Helicopter covers all four dhams with VIP darshan, premium stays and minimal trekking.

Which destinations does CareMyTrip cover?
