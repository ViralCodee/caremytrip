Rosewood Hotels & Resorts is a globally renowned luxury hotel group known for its refined hospitality, personalized service, and unique sense of place. Established in 1979, Rosewood operates ultra-luxury hotels, resorts, and residences in some of the world’s most stunning destinations.

 

Guests enjoy world-class amenities, including Michelin-star dining, holistic wellness at Asaya spas, curated cultural experiences, and unparalleled hospitality. Whether it’s a stay at Hôtel de Crillon in Paris, Rosewood London, or Las Ventanas al Paraíso in Mexico, Rosewood delivers sophistication, exclusivity, and an unforgettable journey.
