Our story

Journeys made with care, since 2013

CareMyTrip.com is a registered travel company based in Dehradun, the gateway to the Himalayas. For over a decade we have helped pilgrims and holidaymakers experience the Char Dham, the hills of Uttarakhand, the backwaters of Kerala and destinations across India and beyond.

We believe that travel is the only thing you buy that makes you richer — so we obsess over the details: comfortable stays, reliable transport, smooth darshan and honest, all-inclusive pricing. From a single Kedarnath yatra to a luxury helicopter Char Dham, our team plans every journey as if it were our own.
